Alisa Padon, PhD

About Alisa

Alisa Padon is an inspiring, dedicated leader in public health research whose passion for evidence‑based policy motivates teams and drives real‑world impact. Her scholarship focuses on harnessing rigorous methods to shape policies that protect communities, reduce health disparities, and create healthier environments for future generations.

With more than 15 years of experience across academia and the nonprofit sector, Alisa has overseen multi‑disciplinary research initiatives, mentored early‑career scholars, and collaborated with government agencies and international partners. Her work has been published in top peer‑reviewed journals and cited by policymakers around the globe.

Outside the lab, you’ll often find Alisa exploring new hiking trails, fostering rescue dogs, or experimenting with bread recipes in the kitchen.
